How Your Donation Helps

Your contribution creates lasting change for children in Papua's mountains, here's how we allocate funds.

School Infrastructure

40% of funds are dedicated to building and maintaining safe, accessible schools closer to remote communities.

Educational Materials

30% provides textbooks, technology, and culturally relevant learning materials to enhance education quality.

Teacher Training

20% supports training local teachers and providing fair compensation to retain qualified educators.
